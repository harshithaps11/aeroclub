  • Vayurvya, a prestigious national-level technical event, commemorates Aeroclub Nitte’s extraordinary decade-long journey. Join us in celebrating noteworthy achievements, groundbreaking innovations, and impactful contributions marking the ten-year expedition of Aeroclub-Nitte. It's not merely an event; it's a celebration of passion, a nexus of ideas, and an invitation to be part of a nationwide community of visionaries and innovators

    This distinguished national-level technical event is the convergence of aviation and technology, tailored for tech enthusiasts, students, professionals, and our expansive community.
