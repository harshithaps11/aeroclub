The "Vayurvya Rotorcraft" Contest offers students in aerospace engineering a valuable platform to develop hands-on skills. By challenging participants to design and build multirotor UAVs, the competition addresses mission-specific requirements, fostering problem-solving and innovation. It emphasizes project management and teamwork, reflecting the complexities of real-world aerospace projects. The practical experience gained, coupled with judging and presentation components, prepares students for the dynamic nature of the aerospace industry, providing a strong foundation for their future careers.

Problem
Statement

The objective for the Rotorcraft Design Challenge is to design, build, and fly a multirotor UAV with a maximum weight limit of 2.5kg and overall dimensions not exceeding 1.5m. The challenge encourages participants to showcase innovation, technical proficiency, and problem-solving capabilities in designing a functional and creative multirotor drone within these constraints.
For detailed guidelines and additional information, please refer the official rulebook.
